How to Play Minesweeper

Minesweeper is a classic puzzle game where your goal is to uncover all the tiles on the grid without triggering any mines.
Use the numbers on the revealed tiles to deduce the locations of hidden mines.

Rules

  • Objective: Clear the grid without clicking on any mines.
  • Clues: Numbers on tiles indicate how many mines are adjacent to that tile.

How to Play

  1. Start the Game: Click on any tile to begin. The first click is always safe.
  2. Revealing Tiles:
    • Left-click on a tile to uncover it.
    • If the tile contains a number, use it as a clue.
    • If the tile is empty, adjacent tiles are automatically revealed.
  3. Marking Mines:
    • Right-click on a tile to flag it as a potential mine.
    • Use flags strategically to avoid accidentally clicking on mines.
  4. Winning the Game:
    • Win by uncovering all non-mine tiles

Difficulties

Different difficulties:

  • Easy: 6 total mines.
  • Medium: 12 total mines.
  • Hard: 20 total mines.

Have fun and good luck uncovering the board!
